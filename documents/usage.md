# TCS User Guide & Menu Reference

## 1. Overview
TCS is a dynamic scenario generator that allows you to spawn training and combat encounters on demand. It is **Session-based**, meaning you and your flight members share the same targets, threats, and scoring.

### Key Concepts
*   **Sessions**: You are always in a session, which defaults to your player callsign (e.g., "Viper 1-1", "Stampede"). You can join other named sessions (ALPHA, BRAVO, CHARLIE) to fly with others. The **Session Lead** controls spawns and difficulty.
*   **Anchors**: Spawns are generated relative to the Session Lead's position and heading.
*   **Difficulty**: Controlled by the Session Lead. Affects enemy skill, aircraft type, and force size.

---

## 2. Getting Started
1.  **Open F10 Radio Menu** > **TCS**.
2.  (Optional) Go to **ADMIN > SESSION** to join a shared session (e.g., "Join ALPHA") or check **Status**.
3.  (Optional) Go to **ADMIN > SESSION > Set Difficulty** to choose your threat level (Default is **Standard**).
4.  Select a module (**TRAINING**, **A2A**, **A2G**, **SUW**) and choose an action.

## 3. Menu Reference

### TRAINING
*Low-threat environments for skill building. Spawns are immediate and close.*

#### Air (A2A)
*   **ACM (Guns Only)**: Close-in dogfighting.
    *   **H2H (Neutral)**: Spawns 1 bandit head-on at 8km (4.3 NM).
    *   **Abeam (Offensive)**: Spawns 1 bandit abeam you.
    *   **Defensive**: Spawns 1 bandit on your six (1.6km / 0.9 NM).
*   **BFM (Missiles)**: Maneuvering fights with heaters.
    *   **Fox 2 (WVR)**: Head-on merge, IR missiles allowed.
    *   **BVR Intercept**: Spawns a BVR target at range (40-80 NM).
*   **Target Drone**: Spawns a non-maneuvering A-10A drone (Weapon Hold).

#### Ground (A2G)
*Spawns a target range ~5 NM ahead of you. Content depends on if you are over Land or Water.*

*   **Bombs**: Static targets for precision or area bombing.
    *   *Land*: Container rows, 476 Range circles, or random grids.
    *   *Water*: Rows of stationary cargo ships.
*   **Guns**: Strafe targets.
    *   *Land*: Soft trucks, armored rows, or 476 strafe pits.
    *   *Water*: Rows of speedboats.
*   **Rockets**: Area targets (soft trucks/grids).
*   **Missiles**: Moving or emitting targets.
    *   **Convoy Hunt**: Moving column of trucks/armor.
    *   **Moving Armor**: Moving tanks/IFVs.
    *   **Pop-up SAM**: Short-range IR SAMs (Osa/Strela) that activate when you get close.
    *   **Radar Emitter**: AAA Radar (Shilka) for SEAD practice.
    *   *Water*: **Naval SAM** (Albatros corvette).
*   **Mixed**: Random scatter of various target types.
*   **Reset Range**: Deletes the current range targets.

---

### A2A (Combat Operations)
*Full-scale air combat scenarios. Spawns are further out and may include multiple waves.*

*   **Intercept**: Spawns a random bandit group (40-80 NM out) heading toward you. May include bombers with escorts.
*   **CAP**: Starts a Combat Air Patrol scenario. You defend a zone while waves of enemies attack it.
*   **Sweep**: Offensive counter-air. You push into a corridor (60 NM long) to clear it of enemies.
*   **Escort**: Spawns a friendly "Package" (e.g., B-52s, Strike Eagles) that flies a route. You must protect them from enemy interceptors.
    *   **Random**: Picks a random package type.
    *   **Strike / CAS / HVAA / Heavy**: Select specific package types to escort.
*   **BVR Random**: Quick-start BVR intercept (similar to Training BVR).
*   **ACM H2H**: Quick-start Dogfight (similar to Training H2H).

---

### A2G (Ground Operations)
*Tactical ground missions. Spawns ~30 NM ahead.*

*   **BAI (Interdiction)**: Spawns a dispersed enemy force (Armor/Mech Inf) moving on roads.
*   **CAS (Close Air Support)**: Spawns Friendly forces in combat with Enemy forces. You must identify and destroy the enemy without hitting friendlies.
*   **Laser On**: If a JTAC is active, requests a laser designation on the current target.
*   **SEAD**: Spawns an Air Defense network (AAA, IR SAMs, Radar SAMs).
*   **DEAD**: Spawns Air Defenses protecting High-Value Targets (Armor/HQ).
*   **Strike**: Spawns a fixed target area (Bunkers, Outposts, Armor) for pre-planned strikes.
*   **Logistics**: Spawns a friendly supply convoy moving from the rear to the front.

---

### SUW / MARITIME (Naval Operations)
*   **Anti-Ship**: Spawns a Surface Action Group (SAG) + Civilian Traffic. You must identify hostiles (Corvettes/Frigates) among neutral shipping.
*   **Naval Strike / Harbor**: Spawns a defended port/anchorage with docked ships and static defenses.
*   **Convoy / Shipping**: Spawns a moving enemy supply convoy (Cargo ships + Escorts).

---

### TASKING
*   **Refresh Board**: Checks for new dynamic missions.
*   **Repeat Last Tasking**: Re-sends the last 9-line or coordinate message.
*   *(Dynamic List)*: Shows active missions generated by the controller (e.g., "CAS Extraction", "Intercept"). You can **Accept** these to get waypoints.

---

### ADMIN
*   **SESSION**: Manage your group.
    *   **Join [Name]**: Join a shared session.
    *   **Advertise**: Tell server you are looking for wingmen.
    *   **Status**: See who is in your session.
    *   **Pass Lead**: Transfer control to another member.
    *   **Set Difficulty**: Change threat level (see below).
    *   **Leave Session**: Go solo.
*   **Reset All Menus**: Fixes F10 menu if it disappears.
*   **Terminate My Bandits**: **IMPORTANT**. Instantly deletes all units spawned by your session. Use this to reset or end your training.
*   **Cleanup All Spawns**: (Server Admin) Wipes all TCS units from the map.

## 4. Difficulty Levels

Difficulty scales **Unit Type**, **AI Skill**, and **Force Size**.

| Level | Label | A2A Threat | A2G/Naval Force Size |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A** | **Beginner** | **Old Gen**: MiG-21, F-5, MiG-23.<br>Weapons: Guns & Rear-aspect IR only.<br>Ratio: 1 bandit per 2 players. | **Platoon / Patrol**<br>Small groups, light armor, no radar SAMs. |
| **G** | **Standard** | **4th Gen**: MiG-29, Su-27.<br>Weapons: All-aspect IR + Fox-1 (SARH).<br>Ratio: 1 bandit per player. | **Company / Task Unit**<br>Medium groups, mixed armor, IR SAMs. |
| **H** | **Advanced** | **Modern**: Su-30, J-11A, F-14, F-16, F-18.<br>Weapons: Fox-3 (Active Radar) allowed.<br>Ratio: 1 bandit per player. | **Battalion / Task Group**<br>Large force, heavy armor, Radar SAMs (Osa/Tor). |
| **X** | **Expert** | **Boss**: MiG-31, Modern Flankers.<br>Weapons: Long-range Fox-3, high skill AI.<br>Ratio: 1 bandit per player. | **Brigade / Task Force**<br>Massive force, high-threat SAMs (Tunguska/Tor), Cruisers. |
